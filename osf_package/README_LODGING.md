# OSF Lodging Package — RISP pre-registrations

**Purpose:** create the external timestamp that converts this project's
pre-registration record from self-attested to verifiable. Everything below
is ready; your task is ~30 minutes of clicks.

## Steps

1. Go to https://osf.io → sign in (create account with your @upenn.edu
   email if needed).
2. Create a new project: title "RISP: Regime-Invariant Specialist Pools —
   pre-registrations and forward tests". Keep it **private for now** (it
   can be made public or view-only-linked later; the timestamp exists
   either way).
3. Upload the three files in this folder (OSF Storage → Upload):
   - `PREREG_FRENCH49_snapshot_2026-07-15.md` (the full A/B/C/D record
     with addenda — refutations included; the refutation record is the
     credibility asset, do not trim it)
   - `PREREG_CRSP.md` (the forward test — the single most valuable
     timestamp in the project)
   - `PREREG_NBER_FRENCH49.md` (the WRDS-independent forward test —
     MUST be lodged before we ever join NBER dates to any panel)
4. Optional but stronger: OSF → Registrations → "New registration" →
   "Open-Ended Registration", paste the abstract of PREREG_CRSP.md, and
   register it. A formal OSF registration is frozen and DOI-stamped —
   the gold standard. (The simple file upload already gives a usable
   timestamp if you skip this.)
5. Copy the project URL (and DOI if you registered) and send them to me —
   they go into the papers' "lodged externally" sentences, and the NBER
   experiment unfreezes the moment this exists.

## Why the rush

- The ICAIF paper (due Aug 2) upgrades from "pre-specified in the
  repository (commit hash)" to "registered (OSF)" only if this is lodged
  before the draft freezes (~Jul 25).
- The NBER-labeler experiment — our WRDS-independent shot at a resolved
  ex-ante prediction — is frozen until this exists, and the Sep 1
  portfolio decision needs its result.
- A timestamp created after the data is touched is worth nothing, and no
  later effort can recreate it.
